# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I'm Yash. Computer science got me in the door, but cybersecurity made me stay. I started on the offensive side. CTFs and HackTheBox drew me in because breaking something is the fastest way to understand how it works. It beats reading documentation.

But attacking a system only gives you half the picture. I started caring just as much about the other side. I wanted to know why a detection rule catches one misconfiguration but misses another, or why some vulnerabilities get flagged in a report while others slip through. That overlap pushes me toward purple teaming instead of pure offense or pure defense. The CJCA certification sits right in that space, which is why I chose it. I am working through it right now.

That same instinct points me towards Governance, Risk, and Compliance long term. I want to know how a system fails and what should have stopped it. Understanding how systems break is one skill. Deciding what the rules should be in the first place is a different one. That is what I am building toward.
